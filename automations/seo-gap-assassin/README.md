# SEO Gap Assassin

Find keywords your competitors rank for that you don't. Get actionable content briefs.

## What It Does

1. Analyzes your top 10 competitors
2. Extracts their ranking keywords
3. Identifies gaps where you're missing
4. Prioritizes by volume and difficulty
5. Generates content briefs for top 20 gaps

> "Cross-references with your existing content, identifies the gaps, and generates actionable briefs."

## Requirements

- `web_search`, `web_fetch`, `file_system` capabilities
