# Review Farm Defense System

Monitor reviews everywhere. Auto-thank positive reviewers. Instant alerts on negative with empathetic response drafts.

## What It Does

- Monitors G2, Capterra, TrustPilot, Product Hunt, Twitter
- Positive reviews: Auto-thank within 2 hours, extract for testimonials
- Negative reviews: Instant alert, draft empathetic response, tag team, log to tracker

> "Response time dropped from 48 hours to 2 hours. Negative reviews get addressed before they spread."

## Requirements

- `web_fetch`, `messaging`, `file_system` capabilities
