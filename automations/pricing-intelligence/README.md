# Pricing Page Intelligence

Never be surprised by competitor pricing changes again.

## What It Does

- Monitors competitor pricing pages every 6 hours
- Detects any changes (prices, tiers, features)
- Analyzes positioning shifts
- Recommends your response

> "When Competitor A drops their pro tier by 20%, you know within hours."

## Requirements

- `web_fetch`, `messaging`, `file_system` capabilities
