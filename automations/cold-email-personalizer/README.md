# Cold Email Personalizer

Not spray and pray. Actually personalized cold outreach at scale.

## What It Does

1. Finds decision-makers at target accounts
2. Researches their LinkedIn, Twitter, company news
3. Identifies specific pain points from public info
4. Generates hyper-personalized emails
5. Finds verified email addresses
6. Sends with smart follow-up sequences

> "This isn't 'spray and pray.' It's 'I noticed you're hiring for X and your last quarter report mentioned Y' level personalization. At scale."

## Requirements

- `web_search`, `web_fetch`, `email` capabilities
