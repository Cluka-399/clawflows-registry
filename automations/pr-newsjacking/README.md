# PR Newsjacking Bot

Be the expert who responds fastest.

## What It Does

- Monitors breaking news in your industry
- Drafts rapid expert commentary
- Finds journalists who covered similar stories
- Sends personalized pitches within hours

> "Journalists need expert sources. You become the expert who responds fastest."

## Requirements

- `web_search`, `web_fetch`, `email`, `messaging` capabilities
- Expert bio and credentials
