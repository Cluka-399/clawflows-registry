# Support-to-Content Pipeline

Turn support headaches into content wins.

## What It Does

- Analyzes support tickets for patterns
- Identifies top questions and frustrations
- Generates content briefs for help articles and blog posts
- Prioritizes by support load reduction potential

> "40% of tickets mention confusion about X feature. Here's a tutorial draft that addresses the top 5 misunderstandings."

## Requirements

- `file_system`, `messaging` capabilities
- Support ticket export (CSV/JSON)
